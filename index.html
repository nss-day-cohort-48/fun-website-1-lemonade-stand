There’s a lot that goes into making a webpage work. If you start by learning the building blocks of laying out your page (HTML or Hyper Text Markup Language) and styling it to look great on any device (CSS or Cascading Style Sheets), you’ll have a lot of flexibility in how your page looks.

Add in using Command Line on your terminal to speed up the work and you’ll have powerful tools at your disposal.
